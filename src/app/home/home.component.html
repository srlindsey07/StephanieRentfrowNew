About Me

"Design is a funny word. Some people think design means how it looks. But of course, if you dig deeper, it's really how it works." - Steve Jobs

My name is Stephanie Rentfrow. While I enjoy solving problems and finding ways to organize information to make it more digestible, I also enjoy front-end development.

My background is in Graphic Design. The company I interned with in college hired me for magazine layouts and ended up teaching me the very basics of front-end development (a small amount of HTML and CSS). From that point I always enjoyed development as well as design and try to use both skills as much as possible.
